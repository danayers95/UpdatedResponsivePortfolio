About me

I'm a server turned web-developer. In the past four months, I've built a portfolio of various applications using HTML, Javascript, CSS, Bootstrap, Heroku and various others.

I've always been a big fan of puzzles and challenges, so the transition into coding and web-development hasn't been too big of a leap for me - I've always felt encouraged by a challenge rather than discouraged.

I'm a drummer, writer, and part-time welder. I've toured the country three times with various bands of various genres of music, and in my free time I can frequently be found playing music.

It's my goal to be able to consistently better myself with my programming and development. In a short time, I've developed a passion for creating intuitive, sleek applications that make a user's life easier.
